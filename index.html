    OpenAI · GTM top 5 challenges · Claude × Gemini synthesis

    Where the GTM team is actually stuck.

    1
    Infrastructure reality — clean data + a controlled orchestration layer You’d own this
    The prerequisite for everything agentic. Feed agents messy data and they don’t fail loudly — they get it wrong quietly, at scale. Meanwhile every tool ships its own agent and wants to be the orchestrator, selling autonomy rather than interoperability.
    EvidenceMulti-source enrichment lifted coverage from the low-40s to high-80s%; the team built its internal data dictionary using OpenAI’s own models. — Keith Jones, OpenAI
    Claudeconsolidated per Gemini
    2
    Motion collision — next-best-action arbitration You’d own this
    With ~3,500 possible motions, executing one is easy; the hard part is deciding which wins for a given user right now, without stacking asks or breaking trust. (See the Operating map tab for the scale of the problem.)
    Evidence28 products × 14 actions × 9 priorities = 3,528 motions; multiple agents can fire on the same logged-in account.
    Geminisharpest add
    3
    Evals for non-deterministic GTM logic You’d own this
    You can’t unit-test an LLM in a Salesforce sandbox. Agentic changes need eval harnesses before they touch live revenue, or they quietly hallucinate routing and discount logic.
    EvidenceOpenAI’s inbound assistant used an eval loop to take first-email accuracy from 60% → 98%. (Not “Symphony” — that’s a Codex coding-agent orchestrator, not a GTM tool.)
    Geminievidence fixed by Claude
    4
    Expansion & retention vs. a stronger-on-enterprise Anthropic Strategic context
    The motion is shifting from land-grab to keep-and-grow at the worst competitive moment — and the expansion/retention systems are the least-built part of the stack.
    EvidenceAnthropic ~$47B run-rate (mid-May 2026) vs OpenAI ~$33B; retention ≈ 88% Enterprise / 68% Team / 59% Plus; enterprise nearing revenue parity by end-2026.
    Both
    5
    Quote-to-cash under IPO-grade rigor Strategic context
    The spreadsheet era is over. Every quote, contract, and billing pipeline must be instantly auditable while products still ship weekly.
    EvidenceNue (CPQ) and Ironclad (CLM) stood up fast for a multi-currency, multi-jurisdiction business; confidential S-1 filed June 8, 2026.

The AI-native internal layer isn’t a nice-to-have — it’s the only way human capacity keeps pace. This is the why beneath the five, not a sixth item.
